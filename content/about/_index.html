About Me

Hello, my name is Wanda Winnicki. I was asked to teach yoga at women's retreat. I used some of the breath prayers and a scripture with yoga poses. I began teaching God in Yoga classes after completing yoga teacher training. I am now a Registered Yoga Teacher with the Yoga Alliance.

I attended Symmetry Yoga Vinyasa Flow 200+ hour teacher training, so I can teach yoga safely.

Yoga helps people seek flexibility, stability and the self confidence that yoga brings with it. The spiritual element of yoga is a powerful part of my life both on and off the yoga mat and I hope that I can bring you the joy of both God and yoga.
