St. John's United Methodist Church This church is located at 28 Cateract Ave. in Dover, NH. God in Yoga originated at St. John's UMC. Lydia's House of Hope This organization is a transitional housing program. Where homeless women with children go from homeless to wholeness with their 12 month residentail program. Donations are collected during God in Yoga classes at St. John's UMC for Lydia's House of Hope.
